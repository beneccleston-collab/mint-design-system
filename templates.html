Usage Guidelines

Consistent Spacing

All templates use our 4px spacing system. Screen padding is typically 20-24px, with 16px gaps between elements.

Component Reuse

Mix and match components from our library. Every element in these screens is documented in the Components section.

Responsive Behavior

These mobile-first templates adapt to different screen sizes while maintaining visual hierarchy and touch targets.
